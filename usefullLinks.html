Useful Links

Kelpwatch.org
This website hosts the world’s largest dynamic map of canopy-forming kelp species, empowering data-driven management and strategic restoration of kelp forest ecosystems.


NASA-funded Projects


Vulnerability of Giant Kelp Populations to Climate Change
A project to develop a global dataset of changes in kelp canopy abundance from the early 1980s to the present.
Using Hyperspectral Imagery to Assess the Effects of Warming on New England Kelp Forests (2020)
The main objectives of this project include developing a methodology to classify kelps using hyperspectral imagery, applying convolutional neural networks to calibrate multi-spectral imagery, and modelling the influence of climate change on kelp abundance.
